### Fact and Info Gathering
- The CDCC scheme has screened 74,900 participants as of May 2025.
- 40% of those screened were diagnosed with prediabetes, diabetes, hypertension, or hyperlipidemia.
- The report highlights the need for better data collection on patient outcomes.

### Issues and Gaps
- Insufficient data on patient follow-up and treatment adherence.
- Unclear metrics for program success.
- Limited engagement with private healthcare providers.

### Questions for Government
- How is patient follow-up being tracked?
- What metrics are used to evaluate the scheme's effectiveness?
- How will private sector collaboration be improved?

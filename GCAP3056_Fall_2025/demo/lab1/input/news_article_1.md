### Fact and Info Gathering
- The Hong Kong government launched the Chronic Disease Co-care Pilot Scheme in November 2023.
- Target: Residents aged 45+ without known diabetes or hypertension.
- As of May 2025, 131,200 enrolled, 74,900 completed screenings, 31,100 diagnosed with chronic conditions.
- Main goal: Early prevention, identification, and treatment of chronic diseases.

### Issues and Gaps
- Participation rate is lower than expected.
- Lack of awareness among target population.
- Limited data on follow-up care and long-term outcomes.

### Questions for Government
- What strategies are in place to increase participation?
- How will follow-up care be ensured for diagnosed individuals?
- Are there plans to expand the scheme to younger age groups?
